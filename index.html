About Us

Heaven Old Age Care Centre, located in Bhandup West, Mumbai, is a dedicated community service initiative focused on the care and well-being of senior citizens.

We provide a calm, secure, and family-like environment where elderly people can live with dignity. Our team ensures emotional support, companionship, and respect for every individual.

Our goal is not just care, but to create a meaningful life filled with joy, belonging, and peace during the golden years.

Our Services

Comprehensive care designed for comfort and dignity

Medical Care

Regular health check-ups, medication support, emergency assistance, and monitoring to ensure physical safety and wellness.

Experienced doctors and caregivers are always available for support.

Daily Assistance

Help with daily activities such as bathing, walking, meals, and personal hygiene with patience and compassion.

Ensuring comfort while promoting independence wherever possible.

Emotional Support

Counseling, companionship, group discussions, and emotional care to reduce loneliness and stress.

Creating a loving environment that feels like home.

Our Activities

Keeping minds active and hearts happy

Yoga & Meditation

Daily yoga and meditation sessions improve flexibility, peace of mind, and emotional balance.

Recreation & Games

Indoor games, music, celebrations, and social events to encourage joy and bonding.

Health Programs

Health camps, awareness programs, and wellness sessions for a healthy lifestyle.
